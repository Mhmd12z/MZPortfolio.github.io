About Me!

My name is Mohammad Zeidan, a passionate 20-year-old studying Computer Science at LIU University. I am highly motivated to pursue a career as a Frontend Developer, with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Even before attending university, I delved into these technologies and gained hands-on experience.

I have successfully completed several website projects, including a responsive CancerCare donation website that adapts seamlessly to various devices. By utilizing only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I demonstrated my ability to create engaging and accessible web interfaces. Additionally, I developed a restaurant website featuring an interactive menu and an intuitive order form, showcasing my practical skills in frontend development.

In addition to my frontend expertise, I possess proficiency in a range of essential tools and technologies. I am adept at using Microsoft applications such as Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, enabling effective documentation and presentation of projects. Furthermore, I have a strong grasp of C#, Microsoft SQL Server, MySQL, and Java, further enhancing my versatility and ability to contribute to diverse development projects.

Driven by my passion for technology, I am continually expanding my skill set and keeping up with the latest industry trends. I am eager to contribute my expertise, creativity, and problem-solving abilities to a dynamic team in a challenging Frontend Developer role.
